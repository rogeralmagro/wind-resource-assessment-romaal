1. Who will author/review which feature branches?
Maria will act as the reviewer for all feature branches.
Albert and Roger will be the authors and responsible for implementing the required changes and tests.

2. How are you collaborating during development, if at all? E.g., pair programming?
We divide the tasks individually according to the agreed plan. Each member works independently on their assigned feature branch. We coordinate regularly and meet on Google Meet to align progress and ensure consistency before opening pull requests.

3. How will you review the PRs? E.g., will you meet as a team to review PRs, or...?
All pull requests will be reviewed synchronously in a Google Meet session. During the meeting, we will go through the code changes together, discuss design decisions, verify functionality and ensure that the tests pass.

4. What is the internal deadlines for accomplishing which tasks?
We set an internal deadline one day before the official submission deadline. On that day, we will meet to review all code and diagrams, run all tests, verify that the repository is clean and correctly structured and ensure everything meets the assignment requirements.

5. What will you do if a team member does not meet their deadline?
If a team member is unable to meet their deadline, they will communicate this as early as possible. The team will redistribute tasks if necessary to ensure timely completion. We are all equally committed to contributing fairly to the workload.

6. How are you communicating?
We communicate primarily via Microsoft Teams and WhatsApp for quick coordination. Formal reviews and technical discussions are conducted via Google Meet.
